Our Mission Against Drugs

The Kerala Police is committed to eradicating drug abuse and trafficking throughout the state. Our comprehensive approach combines enforcement, education, and rehabilitation to address the drug problem from all angles.

We work closely with communities, schools, and families to raise awareness about the dangers of drug abuse and to provide support for those affected by addiction. Our dedicated officers are trained to identify and respond to drug-related issues effectively.

Through our coordinated efforts with local organizations, healthcare providers, and government agencies, we are making significant progress in reducing drug abuse and creating safer communities for all residents of Kerala.

Our Journey

2018

Campaign Launch

The "Say No To Drugs" campaign was officially launched by the Kerala Police to combat rising drug abuse among youth.

2019

School Outreach Program

Expanded our reach to over 500 schools across Kerala, educating students about the dangers of drug abuse.

2021

Rehabilitation Partnership

Formed partnerships with 50+ rehabilitation centers to provide comprehensive support for recovery.

2023

Digital Campaign

Launched digital awareness campaigns reaching millions through social media and online platforms.

2025

Expanding Horizons

Currently working on international collaborations to combat cross-border drug trafficking.
